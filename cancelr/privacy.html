Privacy Policy

Cancelr runs completely on your device. It uses an embedded WebView to log in to your Amazon Subscribe & Save page and sends the same cancellation requests you could perform manually in a browser.

  • No personal data is collected, transmitted, or stored on our servers.
  • All cookies remain inside the app’s WebView and never leave your phone.
  • Ads are delivered by Google AdMob; their SDK may collect device identifiers for advertising and analytics (see Google Privacy Policy).

Data Deletion Request

Because Cancelr stores data only on your device, you can delete everything at any time:

  1. Open Settings › Apps › Cancelr on your Android device.
  2. Tap Storage & cache › Clear storage (or Clear data on some devices).
  3. Alternatively, uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data immediately.
